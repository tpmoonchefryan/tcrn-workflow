# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation respectful and harassment-free for everyone,
regardless of background, identity, experience, or personal characteristics.

## Expected behavior

Be constructive, assume good intent while verifying claims, respect privacy,
and focus criticism on work and decisions. Harassment, threats, doxxing,
discrimination, and unwanted sexual attention are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository maintainers. Maintainers
may remove content, restrict participation, or take other proportionate action.
Reports will be handled with discretion, subject to legal and safety needs.

This policy is adapted from the Contributor Covenant 2.1 principles without
incorporating external contact details.
